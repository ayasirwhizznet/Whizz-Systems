A Legacy of Excellence, A Future of Innovation

Delivering Advanced Electronics Solutions Since 1989

Whizz Systems is a leading provider of electronic product design, development, and manufacturing services, proudly located in the heart of Silicon Valley. Since our inception in 1989, we’ve transformed from a specialized PCB design team into a multidisciplinary powerhouse, bringing deep expertise across engineering and design, new product introduction (NPI), advanced manufacturing, lab testing solutions, sustainable engineering, and end-to-end supply chain management.

We serve a diverse range of industries—including defense and aerospace, medical devices, automotive, consumer electronics, AI computing, and telecommunications—earning the trust of both Fortune 100 companies and innovative startups. For over 30 years, we’ve been the go-to partner for turning groundbreaking ideas into market-ready products, delivering exceptional quality, reliability, and innovation at every stage of the product lifecycle.
