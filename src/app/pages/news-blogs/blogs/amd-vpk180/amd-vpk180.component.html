AMD VPK180 Evaluation Board — Versal Premium Adaptive SoC Platform

A single, versatile evaluation board engineered by Whizz Systems to demonstrate AMD Versal™ Premium capabilities across optical communications, data center acceleration, aerospace, and testing/measurement.

Challenges

Deliver a single board that convincingly demonstrates the capabilities of AMD's Versal Premium Adaptive SoC across multiple high-reliability markets.

  • • Showcasing full Versal Premium SoC performance across telecom, datacenter, aerospace, and test/measurement.
  • • Integrating optical transceivers and multiple form-factor ports while ensuring signal integrity and thermal stability.
  • • Supporting 100G/400G/800G interfaces and IEEE (eCPRI) compliance.
  • • Optimization for manufacturability and developer ergonomics.

Whizz Systems' Contribution

Hardware Architecture & Connectivity

We designed and validated a production-ready board centered on AMD's Versal Premium SoC with high-speed optics and multiple expansion options.

  • • AMD XCVP1802 Versal Premium Adaptive SoC integration and bring up.
  • • 112G PAM4 transceivers, routed for optimal SI.
  • • SFP-DD, QSFP-DD, QSFP and FMC+ connectivity for flexible evaluation.
  • • Support for 100GAUI / 400GAUI / 800GAUI profiles.
  • • 1 Gbps RGMII management Ethernet & 12GB LPDDR4.

Validation & Compliance

Extensive cross-discipline validation: electrical, signal integrity, thermal, and manufacturability checks to meet AMD's standards.
